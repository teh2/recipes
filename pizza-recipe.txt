Ingredients:
- 1 cup warm water
- 1 packet yeast
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 1 can tomato paste
- 1 can tomato sauce
- spices
- toppings (to taste)
- mozarella cheese (or other cheese, to taste)

Dough directions:
1. combine water and yeast. Stir thoroughly.
2. add salt, sugar, and oil. Stir thoroughly.
3. let rest 7 minutes.
4. mix in flour.
5. knead until smooth.
6. let rise, to taste.

Sauce directions:
1. combine paste and sauce in a bowl.
2. add spices to taste. At our house, this means oregano, basil, thyme, garlic powder, onion flakes, red pepper flakes (sparingly).
3. stir thoroughly.

Assembly directions:
1. roll out dough on pizza stone, or cookie sheet.
2. add a layer of sauce.
3. add toppings to taste.
4. add a layer of cheese.
5. bake until crust and cheese begin to brown
6. Eat!
